Classic Waffles

You can never go wrong with this breakfast classic!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 Tsp salt
  • 2 Tbsp white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups of warm milk
  • 1/3 cup of melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar then side aside
  2. Preheat waffle iron to a desired temperature
  3. Separate bowl and beat the eggs
  4. Stir in milk, butter, and vanilla
  5. Beat mixture unti blended
  6. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron
  7. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp then serve
