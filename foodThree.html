Greek Style Garlic Chicken Breast

A family recipe by Michael if you're looking for something greek

Ingredients

  • 4 Boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 Cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 Tsps crushed garlic
  • 1 Tbsp salt
  • 1 1/2 Tsp black pepper
  • 1/3 Tsp paprika

Steps

  1. Cut 3 slits in each chicken breast to allow marinade to penetrate
  2. In a small bowl, whisk togehter olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt, pepper, and paprika for half a minute
  3. Place chicken in a large bowl, and pour marinade over
  4. Cover, and refrigerate overnight
  5. Preheat grill to medium heat, and lightly oil grate
  6. Cook chicken on grill until meat is no longer pink and juices run clear
