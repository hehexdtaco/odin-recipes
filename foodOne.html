Celerita

A refreshing alcoholic beverage for a hot summer.

Ingredients

  • 4 Tbsp chopped celery
  • 1 Tsp white sugar
  • 3 fl oz tequila
  • 12 fl oz lime flavored carbonated water

Steps

  1. Stir celery and sugary together until crushed
  2. Add tequila and stir again to further dissolve sugar
  3. Strain into rock glasses
  4. Add seltzer and enjoy
