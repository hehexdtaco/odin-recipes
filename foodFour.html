Vanilla Ice Cream

The king of flavors with no eggs

Ingredients

  • 3/4 Cup of white sugar
  • 1 Cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 1/4 cups milk
  • 2 Tsps vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Stir sugar, cream, and milk into saucepan over low heat until sugar dissolved
  2. Heat until mix is hot and a small ring of foam appears around the edges
  3. Transfer cream mixture to pourable container like a measuring cup
  4. Stir in vanilla extract and chill mix throughly at least 2 hours or overnight if possible
  5. Pour cold ice cream mix into an ice cream maker
  6. Turn on machine and churn according to manufacturer's directions for 20 - 25 minutes
  7. When ice cream is softly frozen, serve immediately or place plastic wrap over ice cream
  8. Place in freezer to ripen for 2 - 3 hours
